# 📊 HR Employee Attrition Analysis

![CI logo](https://codeinstitute.s3.amazonaws.com/fullstack/ci_logo_small.png)

---

## 📌 Overview
This project delivers a comprehensive data analytics solution focused on understanding and reducing employee attrition. Using the HR Employee Attrition dataset, the project applies statistical analysis, exploratory data analysis (EDA), and interactive dashboard design to uncover key patterns influencing employee turnover.

---

## 📂 Dataset Content
- Demographics: Age, Gender  
- Employment Details: Department, Job Role, Years at Company  
- Compensation: Monthly Income  
- Satisfaction Metrics: Job Satisfaction, Work-Life Balance  
- Target Variable: Attrition (Yes/No)  

---

## 🎯 Business Requirements
- Identify key drivers of employee attrition  
- Analyse attrition trends across departments and roles  
- Assess impact of salary and satisfaction  
- Provide actionable retention insights  

---

## 🔍 Hypothesis and Validation
**H1:** Low job satisfaction increases attrition → Supported  
**H2:** Lower income increases attrition → Partially supported  
**H3:** Poor work-life balance increases attrition → Supported
